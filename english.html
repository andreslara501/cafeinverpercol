About Us

C.I INVERSIONES PERCOL EU is a Colombian family business founded in 2002 in Cartagena de Indias and later transferred to Popayan - Cauca , this initiative is an alternative integration in the production chain threshing specialty coffee for export and / or roasting for national and international consumption.

Special Green Coffee

In global boom of specialty coffees, we offer specialty coffees with cupping valuation certificated by Specialty Coffee Association of America (SCAA), currently, we are promoting origin coffees from departments of Cauca , Huila and Nariño. (Colombia). Rating scales for type blend coffee are in the range of 82, 83 , 84, 85 on the scale SCAA .

Sensory analysis of a specialty coffee

Cupping features:

  • Fragrance / Aroma : hints of fruits (berries, plums, very ripe cherries) notes of caramel and chocolate.
  • Taste: chocolate , Citric Afrutado.
  • Residual Taste: Enduring long ending in sweetness with hints of chocolate and caramel.
  • Acidity: Medium.
  • Body : Medium, lasting and creamy.
  • Uniformity: Uniform Cup
  • Balance: Balanced
  • Cupping : Cupping Clean
  • Sweetness: dark chocolate brown with hints of fruit sugar.

Coffee husks

Coffee husks (CH) are the main solid waste handling and processing of coffee, because for every kilogram of coffee beans produced, generated about 1 kg of husks/shells. Coffee husk allows to make infusions, which gives results in a delicious drink flavored coffee .

Plantation

Craft benefits

Community labour

The coffee is grown by peasant, indigenous and communities, which are in vulnerable situations; they have suffered the consequences of armed conflict in Colombia .

Our company recognizes significant economic value to communities for their coffee.

Small communities contribute to production, results of efforts in coffee growing an excellent product, which is analyzed with high quality standards for excellent offer to customers abroad is obtained.
